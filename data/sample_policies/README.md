# Sample Policy Documents for Testing

Place test policy PDFs here before running the demo.

## Recommended Test Documents (Free to Download)

### Indian Policies
1. **National Education Policy 2020**
   - URL: https://www.education.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/NEP_Final_English_0.pdf
   - Good for: education bias, gender provisions, rural-urban equity

2. **Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023**
   - URL: https://www.meity.gov.in/writereaddata/files/Digital%20Personal%20Data%20Protection%20Act%202023.pdf
   - Good for: socioeconomic access, privacy rights, SDG 16

3. **MGNREGA Act**
   - URL: https://nrega.nic.in/Circular_Archive/archive/MGNREGA_Gazette_EN.pdf
   - Good for: rural bias, wage equity, gender provisions

### International Policies
4. **UN Convention on the Rights of the Child**
   - URL: https://www.ohchr.org/sites/default/files/crc.pdf
   - Good for: age bias, SDG compliance baseline testing
